Details Details Pork & bulgur-stuffed peppers 200 CAL INGREDIENTS NEEDED 4 peppers, halved and cores removed 200g minced pork 1 garlic clove, crushed 2 tsp ground cumin 1 tsp paprika 50g bulgur wheat 250ml vegetable stock
Add to List
DES This colourful feel-good supper brings summer flavour at any time of year, easy to do, 10 mins to prep, 30 mins to cook and sereves 4. How to Cook STEP 1 : Put the peppers, cut-side down, on a plate and microwave on High for 4 mins until cooked through (but not so soft they collapse). If they need longer, microwave for 1 min more and repeat until done. STEP 2 : Put the pork in a cold frying pan and turn on the heat. Fry, breaking up any lumps, until it starts to brown. Stir in the garlic and spices for 1 min, then add the bulgur and stock. Cover and simmer for 10 mins until the bulgur is soft. STEP 3 : Heat the grill. Stir half the parsley into the bulgur, then stuff into the peppers on a baking tray. Grill to crisp, sprinkle over most of the parsley, then serve with the yogurt mixed with remaining parsley.
